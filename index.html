Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. Known for his aggressive batting style and consistency, Kohli is one of the most prolific run-scorers in modern cricket. He has numerous records to his name, including being the fastest to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 runs in One Day Internationals

    

MS Dhoni is a former Indian cricketer and captain, celebrated for his calm demeanor and exceptional leadership skills. Under his captaincy, India won the T20 World Cup in 2007, the 50-over World Cup in 2011, and the Champions Trophy in 2013. Dhoni is renowned for his finishing ability in limited-overs cricket and his lightning-fast wicket

    

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats. Known for his elegant batting style and ability to play big innings, Sharma holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs (264) and has scored three double centuries in the format.
